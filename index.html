Frequently Ask Question

How do we start?                                 

We offer FREE consultation. We meet you up at the site to take the actual measurement. We need to know more about you and your requirements.

What is the normal process of interior design?                                 

We bounce off with a proposed 2D floor plan that comes with a moodboard. Once you are happy with the spacing proposal, we shall proceed to the 3D design.

How long is the interior design process?

Depending on the size of your property, normally it takes about 15 working days for a decent size of condominium.

How much do you charge for design fee?                                 

We are affordable. Our charges is depending on the complexity of your requirement. And we offer discount if you appoint us as the main-con.
